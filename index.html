About Me

Born in Seattle to a commercial fisherman and a practitioner of the Trager® Approach, I grew up loving the ocean and the Northwest. After high school, I decided to spread my wings a bit and received a Bachelors in Liberal Arts with a special interest in Classical Asian Literature from Soka University of America, located in California.

While at Soka University, I studied abroad in China, which inspired me to teach English there after I graduated. Two adventure filled years later, I returned to Seattle to study massage and then the Trager® Approach at Discoverypoint School of Massage, where I later became a member of the faculty.

After five years of making the world a better place one massage at a time, I was ready for a new challenge. The short (and funnier) version of the story: My housemate and I were talking about her job at Microsoft, and she said, "You really liked the Symbolic Logic class at Soka, right?" When I answered in the affermative, she continued, "I have something you should read and see if you like it." A couple of days later, she handed me a textbook about programming in C which I enjoyed so much that I applied to coding bootcamp two weeks later.

To see what I have accomplished at the University of Washington hosted Coding Bootcamp, click on Portfolio above.
