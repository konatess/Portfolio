About Me

Born in Seattle to a commercial fisherman and a practitioner of the Trager® Approach, I grew up loving the ocean and the Northwest. After high school, I decided to spread my wings a bit and received a Bachelors in Liberal Arts with a special interest in Classical Asian Literature from Soka University of America, located in California.

While at Soka University, I studied abroad in China, which inspired me to teach English there after I graduated. Two adventure filled years later, I returned to Seattle to study massage and then the Trager® Approach at Discoverypoint School of Massage, where I later became a member of the faculty.

In the next phase of my journey, I am attending the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at the University of Washington.

Fun Facts About Me:
  • I am an Open Water Certified SCUBA Diver
  • I have visited seven countries outside the U.S. (at last count)
  • I have been to the tourist base camp of Mount Everest
  • I rode a camel in the Gobi Desert, an elephant in Thailand, and a yak on the shore of Namtso (Namtso, also known as Lake Nam, is the largest salt lake in the Tibet Autonomous Region, and the highest salt lake in the world)
  • I was once featured in a 2 minute interview segment on a local TV show broadcast in Zhejiang, China
  • I have freelanced as a copy editor, and have a thank you credit in a published book
