There are a few choose your own adventure creation platforms out there, but most of them aren't very user friendly, especially for people who don't think like coders. We wanted to make a site that is user friendly for people who want some of the great features available on sites like Twine, and don't want to learn special syntax in order to use them. Our site allows users to simply click, drag and type, without the fuss of adding special brackets and other symbols. We also wanted a slick professional look, to further differentiate our product from the sites with amateur or outdated layouts.
